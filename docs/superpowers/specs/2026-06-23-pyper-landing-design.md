# Pyper Landing Page Design

## Goal

Build a single conversion-focused landing page for Pyper in Next.js, using the Stitch export as visual direction and the SEO brief as content direction.

## Audience

Companies that sell, support, or qualify leads through WhatsApp and need to centralize conversations, CRM stages, AI agents, and commercial process automation.

## Page Structure

- Hero with the literal offer: "CRM com IA e WhatsApp para automatizar seu atendimento".
- Pain section focused on slow responses, lost leads, and scattered follow-ups.
- Solution section showing how Pyper centralizes CRM, WhatsApp, AI agents, and automations.
- Process section for lead management, pipeline visibility, and commercial task automation.
- FAQ section with structured data.
- Final CTA that opens WhatsApp directly.

## SEO Requirements

- Primary keyword: "CRM com IA e WhatsApp".
- Secondary keywords used naturally: "CRM com WhatsApp", "agente de IA para WhatsApp", "automação de atendimento", "automação comercial", "gestão de leads", "funil de vendas", and "gestão de processos comerciais".
- Static metadata with title, description, canonical, Open Graph, Twitter Card, and robots.
- Generated `robots.ts` and `sitemap.ts`.
- JSON-LD for Organization, SoftwareApplication, and FAQPage.

## Conversion Requirements

- All primary CTAs open `https://wa.me/558581086339` with the encoded message "Olá, quero agendar uma demonstração da Pyper."
- CTA copy uses "Agendar demonstração" as the main action.
- The page avoids exaggerated promises such as guaranteed revenue multipliers.

## Visual Direction

Use the Stitch "Luminous Precision" direction: bright surface, charcoal text, emerald action color, dense SaaS dashboard cues, thin borders, and restrained motion. The first viewport should clearly signal Pyper and show a product-like CRM/WhatsApp dashboard rather than a generic split hero.
